Help & Support

How do I create a paste?
Click on "Add Paste" in the navigation bar, fill in the title and content, then click upload. You can choose to make it private if you want.
How do I upgrade my account?
Go to the "Upgrades" page and choose a plan that suits your needs. You can pay with crypto or credit card.
Is my data secure?
All pastes are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties.
How do I report content?
Contact us through Telegram or email support@exiled.cc with the paste URL and reason for reporting.
